‚ôø Why Form Labels Matter

Accessibility Impact

  • Screen Readers: Blind users rely on labels to understand form fields. Without labels, screen readers announce "text edit blank" with no context.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Users can tab to inputs and hear the label announced.
  • Click Targets: Proper labels increase clickable area - users can click label text to focus the input.
  • Legal Compliance: WCAG 2.1 Level A requires form labels. ADA compliance depends on proper labeling.
